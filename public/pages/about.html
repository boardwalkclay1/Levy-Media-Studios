About Levy Media Studios

Levy Media Studios is a cinematic media company focused on building story worlds that expose truth and challenge systems — starting with the BAMN Universe and the Preschool to Prison Pipeline (PPP) project.

Who You Are

A creator, strategist, and storyteller who understands both the streets and the systems. Levy Media Studios is your vehicle to turn that understanding into worlds, books, visuals, and impact.

Why You Created BAMN

BAMN was created to tell the truth about survival, loyalty, and power in environments shaped by invisible rules. It’s not just entertainment — it’s a mirror and a warning.

Why PPP Matters

PPP exists to show that the “system” isn’t failing — it’s functioning exactly as designed. By breaking down how kids are funneled from classrooms to cages, PPP positions you as a serious voice in conversations about justice, policy, and reform.

Vision

The vision is a full media empire: books, comics, film, animation, and digital content that all connect back to real-world impact. Levy Media Studios is the hub — BAMN and PPP are just the first pillars.
