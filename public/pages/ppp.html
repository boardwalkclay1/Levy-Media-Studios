What Is Preschool to Prison Pipeline (PPP)?

PPP is a deep dive into how institutions, policies, and incentives work together to push children — especially Black and Brown kids — from classrooms into cages. It’s not an accident. It’s architecture.

Key Themes

  • Education System: How early labeling and discipline shape futures.
  • Policing: Over-surveillance and criminalization of youth.
  • Incarceration: The business model behind mass incarceration.
  • Incentives: Who profits when kids are treated like cases, not humans.

Access the Work

PPP is positioned for educators, organizers, policy makers, and anyone serious about understanding how the pipeline really works.
